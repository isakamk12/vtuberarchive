## [Batch 2] Global & Special Projects

### 07. WACTOR (now 910inc)
- **Status:** Rebranded to 910inc (Jan 2024). Active/Legacy transitions.
- **Concept:** Global pioneer (JP, CN, ES). One of the first JP agencies to achieve massive success in the Spanish-speaking market.
- **Aesthetic:** High-tech / Cyberpunk / Global.
- **Key Members:** ヒヅキミウ (Hizuki Miu), ミツルギリア (Mitsurugi Lia), etc.

### 08. VERSEⁿ (ヴァース)
- **Status:** Merged into VEE (Sony Music Labels) on April 2, 2024.
- **Concept:** "A story about girls who connect the world." Cross-media storytelling across anime, music, and manga.
- **Aesthetic:** Cinematic / Artistic / Ethereal.
- **Members (at merger):** Maru Nanamona, Hitoshiro Itsuki, Kagase Uno, Alba Sera, Kashi Otoha.

### 09. V-Dere
- **Status:** Independent, talent-led group (US-based).
- **Concept:** Friendship-based collaboration without a corporate management layer. Branding centered around "Dere" personality types.
- **Aesthetic:** Character-centric / Dreamy Night.
- **Members:** Airi Viridis, Kirispica, Mozumi Pichi, Alpha Betta, Hani Hima, Tomoe Umari.

### 10. Interlunium
- **Status:** Active Vocal Group (under I MY ME label). Debuted Dec 2017.
- **Concept:** Story-driven vocal group with 11 members. Focus on high-frequency musical releases and subunits (Aromatique, Chi-Bi, etc.).
- **Aesthetic:** Music Studio / Galaxy / Professional Idol.
- **Members:** Junko, Rinko Hanazawa, Kanon Mitsuboshi, Chiyo Watanabe, Dam Bi Han, etc.

### 11. PixelLink
- **Status:** Officially dissolved on March 31, 2025.
- **Concept:** EN-focused virtual talent agency. Had two generations: PixelStart+ and PixelChronicle.
- **Aesthetic:** Retro Pixel Art / Digital Neon.
- **Current Status:** Most members purchased their own IPs to continue as independent creators.

### 12. SpringFish (春魚創意)
- **Status:** Active Major Taiwanese Agency.
- **Concept:** Scientific and thematic storytelling. Includes iconic groups like "Rescute" (Endangered Species) and "Xtreme Deep Field" (Space).
- **Aesthetic:** Marine / Deep Space / Traditional Chinese Luxury.
- **Generations:** Rescute (濒临绝种团), Xtreme Deep Field (极深空计画), Celestial (瑟拉斯蒂欧), Timaeus (谛觅司).

### 13. Hakohako (箱箱The Box / Meridian Project)
- **Status:** Active Major Taiwanese Agency.
- **Concept:** High-quality production and variety. Operates under the "Meridian Project" (子午計画).
- **Aesthetic:** Geometric / Box / Minimalist.
- **Key Member:** 森森鈴蘭 (Lily Linglan).

## [Batch 3] Domestic & Emerging Projects

### 14. ProPro Production (ぷろぽり)
- **Status:** Active/Reorganizing.
- **Concept:** "ProPro" (Professional Production) specializing in female VTubers. Known for distinct character types and specific sub-units.
- **Aesthetic:** Modern Agency / High-end Character Focus.
- **Members:** A variety of generations. Current focus is on maintenance and core talent support.

### 15. Synthmaniacs (シンセマニアックス)
- **Status:** Active (Debuted Sept 2025).
- **Concept:** Music production project using synthesizers as a core motif. Focus on original songs and instrumental performances.
- **Aesthetic:** Electronic Music Studio / Neon Wave.
- **Members:** 譜束ぴあの, 譜束いおり, 刻美りずむ, 波導らいざ, 符洛院カナデ, 天空乃ヒビキ.

### 16. MugenLive (夢幻Live)
- **Status:** Active (Brave Group China subsidiary).
- **Concept:** Multilingual agency primarily on Bilibili. Absorbed VSPO! China members in Oct 2025.
- **Aesthetic:** Danmaku / Streaming Interface / Global.
- **Groups:** eSports Unit (former VSPO! CN), Overseas Solo members.

### 17. GuildB / GuildCQ
- **Status:** Mystery Connection. GuildCQ is the confirmed Quartette project.
- **GuildB** appears to be a legacy placeholder with no distinct corporate footprint separate from the "Guild" circles.
