THE SLUG'S TRANSFORMATION

Once a tiny slug in the ocean, Yui's life changed during a massive thunderstorm. While climbing a high rock to watch the lightning, she was swept away by a wave. When she woke up, she was in her current human-like form and was subsequently adopted by a human family.

PEKORA OSHI #1

TRUE LOVE: PEKORA

Yui calls Pekora her "only true love." She collects merchandise and cards obsessively, but insists: "I am not obsessed or parasocial, she's just my favorite VTuber in the whole world!" Her energy spikes into 'Giga-Yapping' mode whenever the Rabbit is mentioned.

"NO OFFENSE, BUT..."

Management (Senbei) was caught off guard by Yui's strong personality. During their first meeting, Senbei told her: "No offense, but your personality is much stronger than I expected." He repeated "No offense" twice, as if to emphasize his surprise at her gap between appearance and character.

THE DOOR STROKING INCIDENT

"I love stroking the door... it's so sexy. This door is my only saving grace right now."

During a high-stress *Resident Evil 7* stream, Yui descended into a minor madness, stroking the "Safe Room Door" and weeping while professing her love for it as her only protection against the horror.

DECO YUI

A professional cake decorator at heart. In her "Deco Yui" handcam streams, she demonstrates professional-grade speed and precision in decorating cakes, a skill she developed long before becoming a VTuber.

THE 17 MILLION RETAKES

Despite her beautiful singing voice, Yui suffered from extreme lack of confidence. She revealed that she recorded her first cover song nearly "17 million times," dying inside with each retake until she finally heard the mix and realized, "Oh, I actually really like this."

THE BULLET BILL SCANDAL

A bizarre community meme where Yui is accused of having a "scandal" with Mario's Bullet Bill. She frequently has to defend herself: "I did NOTHING to Bullet Bill! He is safe! He is fine! Stop it!"

THE "ROKU NANA" CURSE

Initially annoyed by the "67" (Roku Nana) spam in her chat, Yui has now embraced the curse. "I love it now because it annoys everyone around me, and I like that!"
