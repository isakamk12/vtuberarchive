THE LOL ADDICTION

CURRENT RANK: BRONZE / SILVER

Serena's love for *League of Legends (LoL)* is a source of both joy and terror for her fans. Maining Sona and Seraphine, her obsession reached a point where her "7,000 USD Donation Goal" in her birthdayathon was to STOP playing LoL for one month.

  • LoL Anniversary: Former LoL-addicts reinstalled the game just to play with her on her birthday, a testament to her community's chaotic loyalty.

SUISEI ENTHUSIAST

Inspired directly by Hoshimachi Suisei of Hololive. Hearing "GHOST" was the catalyst for Serena wanting to become a VTuber. She is a deep otaku of *Project Sekai* (An oshi), *Love Live!* (Mari oshi), and *Macross*.

THE WORKAHOLIC CEO

Serena handles nearly all production for her original works. For her debut "Lost in Time," she wrote lyrics, composed the melody, managed harmonies, synchronized timing, and even drew the storyboards for the MV.

  • Vocal Guide Legend: For her birthday concert "Star Eyed Serenade," she created individual guide vocal tracks for every single participant to ensure they could sing comfortably.
MAMA STARSTRUCK'S COMPLIANCE: "3 minutes before my debut, my mom asked if I could wash the dishes. I had to scrub fast before going live."

Serena lives with her mother, who views her activities as "those anime things" and "those Japanese things." Serena jokes that she can't bring her mom on stream because she lacks "Internet Literacy" and would get canceled instantly.

HORROR FRAGILITY

Despite her deity-given powers, Serena is a total coward in horror games. In *Lethal Company*, she is famously the one screaming at the top of her lungs, only to be either babied or abandoned by her colleagues.

THE TOKAKU LINK

Serena's backstory is tied to Tokaku. After Serena lost a friend and wept in despair, Tokaku granted her time-traveling powers. However, Serena's accidental timeline destruction led her to SVP, with Tokaku following closely behind. They constantly bicker over who is the true "older sister" based on who was designed first.
