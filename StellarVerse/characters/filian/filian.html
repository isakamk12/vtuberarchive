LEADERSHIP PROFILE

Filian is the driving force behind Stellar Verse Productions (SVP). Known for her high-octane energy and complete lack of shame, she transitioned from an independent powerhouse to the visionary founder of a global agency.

  • Role: Founder / CEO / Visionary
  • Generation: 0 (SVP Origin)
  • Fans: Fili-army / Snacks
  • Special Skill: VR Acrobatics (Backflips, head-spins, etc.)
  • Catchphrase: "I'm literally the best."

THE VR ASCENSION

Unlike many VTubers, Filian operates primarily in full-body VR. Her streams are a physical workout, involving constant movement, stunts, and occasional "breaking" of her digital avatar in hilarious ways. She is famous for her ability to snap her avatar's neck 180 degrees to scare her audience.

THE SBE DYNAMIC

While Filian is the boss, she shares a legendary dynamic with her manager/CEO Senbei (Sembie). Their interactions often involve Sembie trying to maintain project compliance while Filian pushes every boundary possible. She is the reason SVP exists as a "Chaos Sanctuary."

CHAOTIC LEGACY

  • Collab Champion: Known for being the most energetic and unpredictable guest in any collaboration.
  • Philanthropy: Despite her chaos, she has raised massive amounts for charity, using her high-energy platform for good.
  • The Vision: Created SVP to house talents who were "too weird, too funny, or too chaotic" for traditional agencies.

THE "SNACK" MENTALITY

Filian views her community as both her biggest support and her personal clown troupe. Her streams are a constant back-and-forth of roasting, high-level bit-playing, and physical comedy that has redefined the "VR-Tuber" genre.
