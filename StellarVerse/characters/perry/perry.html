COSMIC LORE & ORIGIN

Originally a business owner in the void of space, Perry used wormholes to help people find lost items. Her journey to Earth began with a tragic nap behind the wheel, leading her to crash directly into the Sun and undergo a "Time-Space Distortion Isekai" into her current idol form.

"I'm 100% Trailer Trash. Back in school, I was so quiet and wore such big coats that people thought I was a drug dealer. I was just crying."

THE WORM MILK WAR

A darker truth lies behind her cute exterior. Perry reveals that "Worm Milk" (tasting like Froot Loops milk or cotton candy) is the most addictive and nutritious substance in the universe. This has led to the "Worm Wars," where worms are kidnapped for milking factories. Perry is currently hiding on Earth to avoid being sent to a factory.

THE SBE CHRONICLES

Perry's relationship with the SVP staff and her CEO (SBE/Sembie) is defined by a desperate craving for fatherly validation. In a famous meeting, she demanded a compliment from SBE, stating:

"I never had a father, so I want to know what it feels like to be validated by one."

This led to her generation-mate Reena claiming she would be Perry's father instead, spiraling into chaotic wholesome energy.
