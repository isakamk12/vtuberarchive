JUST A HUMAN BEING

THE MASK OF HUMANITY

To hide her eldritch owl nature, Rina frequently wears a hat that explicitly states "Just a human being." Despite her best efforts to act "normal," her owl-like tendencies—such as responding with "Hoo?" and her decorative feather ears—often give her away.

"I am 100% a normal human. Please ignore the stomach sounds and the feathers."

THE LEGENDARY MUTE DEBUT

Rina's debut is etched in VTuber history for all the wrong reasons. She spoke for almost the entire duration of her debut stream with her microphone muted, completely oblivious to her audience's panic. This led to the creation of her "Pon Owl" (Pawn Owl) title, representing her endearing clumsiness.

IDOL MANIA & RADIO REBEL

A hardcore enthusiast of *Hello! Project*. Her legendary "Oshiloss" followed the graduation of Eri Kamei, and she currently supports Sakura Oda. Rina is known for her "Radio Rebel" habit: tucking her hair behind her ear and blushing intensely whenever she sees a cute girl.

  • The Oshi Fail: Once shouted the wrong name at a god-tier idol meeting, resulting in her being cut off by staff.

CRASH OUTS & FICTIONAL UNCLE LOVE

Rina often gets emotionally overwhelmed by storytelling. She is known to "Crash Out" for 90% of a stream over fictional characters, specifically "nerdy older men" like Simon Petrikov from *Adventure Time*.

THE DREAMSCAPE REALITY

Her "Dreamscape" setting carries a deep personal meaning. Due to chronic health issues and her role as a caregiver for her parents, Rina is physically confined to her home. Her VTuber identity is her way of connecting with the world when her body cannot. She deeply respects Ironmouse for her similar resilience and charity work, aiming to give back to the community one day.

THE "SENBEI" CONNECTION

Rina has a playful relationship with the SVP CEO, "Senbei." She frequently imitates his deep sighs and catchphrases like "Nice..." or "You want the short story or the long story?" while acknowledging him as a supportive manager who always puts her health first.
